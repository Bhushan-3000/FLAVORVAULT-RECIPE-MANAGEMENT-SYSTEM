Help & Support

Find answers to common questions or contact our support team for assistance.

To add a recipe, simply navigate to the "My Recipes" section and click on the "Add Recipe" button. Fill in the required details such as ingredients, steps, and photos, then click "Save."

Yes, FlavorVault allows you to share recipes with friends and family. Just click on the "Share" button located in the recipe's details page and choose the preferred sharing method.

You can create meal plans by going to the "Meal Planner" section. Select the recipes you want to include, set the days for each meal, and save your plan.

Yes, you can generate a shopping list from selected recipes in the "Shopping List" section. The ingredients from your chosen recipes will be automatically added to the list.

Currently, FlavorVault is designed for online use, but you can export your recipes as PDF files for offline access.

Go to the "Edit Profile" section, select "Change Password," and follow the prompts to reset your password.

To delete a recipe, go to "My Recipes," select the recipe you want to delete, and click the "Delete" button.
